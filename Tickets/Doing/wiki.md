# Doing

The ticket currently being worked. **One at a time** under normal circumstances.

- The agent moves a ticket here from `Backlog/` when picking it up and sets `status: In Progress`.
- The agent appends dated **Work Log** entries here continuously — discoveries, decisions, actions.
- To pause: leave it here; the Work Log is the resume point. `/ticketing-list` surfaces stalled
  Doing/ tickets so nothing is silently abandoned.
- On close the agent moves it to `Done/` (see that folder's wiki for depth rules).
